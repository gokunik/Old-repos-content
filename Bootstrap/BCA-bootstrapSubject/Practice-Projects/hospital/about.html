Who we are and
what we do

Best hospital chain
in the world

Fortis Healthcare Limited is a leading integrated healthcare delivery service provider in India. The healthcare verticals of the company primarily comprise hospitals, diagnostics and day care specialty facilities. Currently, the company operates its healthcare delivery services in India, Nepal, Dubai and Sri Lanka with 36 healthcare facilities (including projects under development), approximately 4,000 operational beds and over 415 diagnostics centres.

AWARDS & ACCOLADES

Economic Times Healthworld Hospital Awards’20 Fortis Group won four awards in various categories at the Economic Times Healthworld Hospital Awards’20. Fortis won the ‘Best Hospital Chain’ and the ‘Best Hospital for Patient Care’ titles nationally. Fortis Hospital, Bannerghatta Road, Bengaluru, was named the ‘Best Hospital - Urology’ in its region. Fortis Memorial Research Institute, Gurugram, was recognised as the ‘Best Hospital - Oncology’ in its region.
